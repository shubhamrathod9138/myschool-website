Choose Your Stream

Explore the streams we offer and find the path that best matches your interests and future goals.

Science Stream

The Science stream is ideal for students interested in engineering, medicine, research, and other technical careers. It includes subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and Computer Science.

Commerce Stream

The Commerce stream prepares students for careers in business, finance, accounting, and management. Core subjects include Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and Mathematics.

Arts Stream

The Arts stream supports students with creative, social, and humanities interests. Popular subjects are History, Geography, Political Science, Psychology, Sociology, and English.

Vocational Subjects

  • Hands-on learning in areas such as IT and business
  • Support for career-focused, skill-based education
  • Enhances employability and practical thinking
