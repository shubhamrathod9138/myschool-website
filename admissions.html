❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our admission process

What is the admission fee?

The admission fee is ₹2,500 for all classes. This is a one-time fee and includes registration, admission form, and initial processing charges.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for outstanding academic performance and need-based financial assistance for deserving students from economically weaker sections.

What are the school timings?

School operates from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM from Monday to Friday. Saturday classes are optional and run from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM for additional academic support.

Is transportation provided?

Yes, we provide safe and reliable transportation facilities covering major areas of Nansad and surrounding villages. Bus routes are designed for convenience and safety.

What extracurricular activities are offered?

We offer a wide range of activities including sports (cricket, football, volleyball, athletics), cultural programs (dance, music, drama), science clubs, debate clubs, and community service programs.

How can I track my application status?

You can track your application status by contacting the admission office at +91-9876543210 or visiting the school office. We also send regular updates via SMS and email.
