School Facilities

Comprehensive infrastructure designed to support academic excellence and holistic student development.

Overview

Our school provides modern facilities including well-equipped classrooms, advanced science and computer laboratories, vocational courses guided by experienced teachers, healthcare and beauty & wellness laboratories, smart boards in every class, a rich library, sports grounds, and activity halls to support academic excellence and overall student development.

Academic Facilities

  • Modern Classrooms: Spacious, well-ventilated learning spaces equipped with contemporary teaching aids
  • Smart Classes: Interactive smart boards and digital learning tools in every classroom
  • Science Laboratories: Well-equipped labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with modern apparatus
  • Computer Laboratory: Advanced computers with internet connectivity for IT education
  • Rich Library: Extensive collection of books, reference materials, and digital resources

Vocational & Specialized Labs

  • Healthcare Lab: Hands-on training in medical fundamentals and healthcare procedures
  • Beauty & Wellness Lab: Practical training in cosmetology, beauty therapy, and wellness practices
  • Other Vocational Courses: Industry-aligned training guided by experienced professional instructors

Sports & Recreation

  • Playground: Large, well-maintained grounds for various outdoor sports and activities
  • Activity Hall: Indoor space for cultural programs, assemblies, and indoor sports
  • Sports Equipment: Modern equipment for cricket, badminton, volleyball, and other activities

Support Services

  • Professional Teaching Staff: Dedicated educators using modern teaching methodologies
  • Student Support: Counseling and guidance services for academic and personal development
  • Infrastructure: Adequate drinking water, sanitation facilities, and safe environment
